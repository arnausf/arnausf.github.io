My name is Victoria Perea, a 25 years old designer from Barcelona.

After my final college work I asked myself, How did I get here? I mean, the theme of my final paper was a direct attack on digital culture and new information technologies and the clearly negative impact of the digital age. Maybe for that reason I decided to dedicate myself to digital work, to be able to do a good practice of it. Thanks to hating technology, I have become an expert in it.
I come from an analog world, from staining myself with ink and fighting with print machines and pdf files, and thats another reason I thought about the digital world, ecology. I can do design without needing any physical materials.
I could tell you all my personal, technical and philosophical journey about my design practices, but no one would read it.
In short, I focus on people, building empathy and improving society with good research. So much talking about technology has made me forget about myself.
